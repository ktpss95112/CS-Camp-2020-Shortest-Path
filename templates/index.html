Rules

Rule 1

Given a starting point A and multiple destination points B, C, ......, find the shortest path of A to B, A to C, ......

Rule 2

Your score will be equal to the sum of Your Path Length - the Actual Shortest Path Length

Rule 3

The team with the smaller score wins the game. Any unsubmitted paths will be counted as a score of 100.

Rule 4

Every round lasts 2.5 minutes and you have unlimited submission attempts (only the best result will be counted)
